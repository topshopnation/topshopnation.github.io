Privacy Policy

HarborCast Marine Weather | Last Updated: June 2026

Summary: HarborCast does not require an account and does not use ads, analytics, or tracking. The app runs on your device using public government weather data. The one exception is optional lightning alerts: if you turn them on, your device's push token and an approximate (city-level) alert location are sent to our alert server solely to notify you of nearby lightning.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

HarborCast is designed with your privacy as a priority. The app:

  • Does NOT require account creation or login
  • Does NOT collect your name, email, or contact information
  • Does NOT transmit your personal data to any server, except the limited data needed for optional lightning alerts you choose to enable (see Section 5)
  • Does NOT use analytics or tracking services
  • Does NOT share any data with third parties

2. Location Data

HarborCast may request access to your device's location to provide weather and tide data for your area. This location is used on your device to fetch publicly available NOAA weather data and is not shared with third parties. If you enable lightning alerts, an approximate (city-level) version of your alert location is sent to our alert server only to deliver those notifications (see Section 5).

You can deny location access and manually enter your preferred locations instead.

3. Data Storage

All data you configure in HarborCast (saved locations, preferences, favorite stations) is stored locally on your device only using the app's local storage.

This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We cannot see or access your information
  • If you delete the app, your saved data is permanently removed

4. Weather Data Sources

HarborCast retrieves publicly available weather, tide, lightning, and marine condition data from public sources including NOAA, the National Weather Service, NDBC buoy stations, and NASA satellite imagery. These weather requests are made directly from your device.

5. Lightning Alerts (Optional)

HarborCast Pro can send push notifications when lightning is detected near you, even when the app is closed. This feature is off by default and turns on only if you enable it.

When you enable lightning alerts, the app sends the following to our alert server (operated for us by Cloudflare) for the sole purpose of delivering those notifications:

  • Your device's Apple push notification token
  • An approximate, city-level alert location, your chosen alert radius, and any quiet hours you set

This information is used only to send you lightning alerts. It is not used for advertising or tracking, is not shared with third parties for their own purposes, and is removed when you turn lightning alerts off. Lightning detection is satellite-based and approximate, and is provided for awareness only, not as a substitute for official warnings.

HarborCast does not display any advertising.

6. In-App Purchases

HarborCast offers optional PRO features via in-app purchase. These purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information such as credit card numbers.

7. Third-Party Services

HarborCast does not use analytics platforms or advertising networks. The only third parties involved are Apple, which processes subscriptions and delivers push notifications, and Cloudflare, which runs the alert server that sends lightning notifications. Neither receives your data for their own marketing purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

HarborCast is a marine weather tool intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

9. Data Security

Because your data never leaves your device, it is as secure as your device itself. We recommend:

  • Using device passcodes or biometric authentication
  • Keeping your device software up to date

10. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data for us to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over your information stored locally on your device.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
