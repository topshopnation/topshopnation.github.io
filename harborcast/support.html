Frequently Asked Questions

Where does HarborCast get its weather data?

HarborCast uses publicly available data from NOAA, the National Weather Service, and NDBC buoy stations to provide marine weather, tide predictions, and sea conditions.

Is my data private?

Yes. All your data stays on your device. We never collect, transmit, or store your personal information. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I restore my PRO purchase?

Open HarborCast and go to Settings. Tap "Restore Purchases" and your PRO features will be restored using your Apple Account.

Can I request a refund?

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and follow Apple's refund process.

Should I rely on HarborCast for safety decisions?

HarborCast is an informational tool. Always check official NOAA and Coast Guard advisories, carry proper safety equipment, and exercise sound judgment before going on the water.
