Privacy Policy

QR Pro Scanner

Last updated: January 2025

QR Pro Scanner does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your privacy is our priority.

Introduction

QR Pro Scanner ("the App") is developed and operated by CoreForge LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The App operates entirely on your device without transmitting data to external servers.

Data Storage

All data created by the App is stored locally on your device:

  • Scan History: Records of QR codes you scan are stored only on your device
  • Created QR Codes: QR codes you generate are stored only on your device
  • Preferences: App settings are stored only on your device

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Camera Access

The App requests camera access solely for the purpose of scanning QR codes and barcodes. We do not:

  • Store or save any images from your camera
  • Transmit camera data to any servers
  • Use camera access for any purpose other than scanning codes

Photo Library Access

The App may request photo library access for two purposes:

  • Scanning from photos: To scan QR codes from images you select
  • Saving QR codes: To save generated QR codes to your photo library

We only access photos you explicitly select and do not browse or analyze your photo library.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and in-app purchases
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management. RevenueCat receives anonymous purchase data from Apple to manage your subscription status. View their privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy

In-App Purchases

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID credentials.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The App is suitable for all ages as it does not collect any personal data.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, your information is protected by your device's built-in security features, including device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
