1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

NestPlan is designed with your privacy as a priority. The app:

2. Data Storage

All data you enter into NestPlan (account balances, ages, projections, scenarios) is stored locally on your device only using your browser's or app's local storage.

This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device

  • If you delete the app or clear browser data, your saved scenarios are permanently removed
