Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my PRO purchase on a new device?
Open NestPlan, go to the PRO section, and tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore any previous purchases linked to your Apple ID.
Where is my data stored?
All your data (account balances, scenarios, settings) is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to your financial information. This also means if you delete the app, your data will be lost – consider exporting your scenarios first (PRO feature).
How accurate are the projections?
NestPlan uses standard financial calculations and current tax brackets. However, projections are estimates based on assumptions that may not match reality. Market returns vary, tax laws change, and personal circumstances differ. Always consult a financial advisor for personalized advice.
What is Monte Carlo simulation?
Monte Carlo simulation runs 1,000 different scenarios with randomized market returns to show the range of possible outcomes for your retirement plan. It helps you understand the probability of your savings lasting through retirement, accounting for market volatility.
What's included in PRO?
PRO features include:
  • Ad-free experience – No banner ads or interstitials
  • Monte Carlo simulation (1,000 runs)
  • Total Assets Over Time chart
  • Asset Allocation chart
  • Year-by-Year projection table
  • PDF export with full reports
  • JSON import/export for backups
  • Up to 12 saved scenarios

Pricing is available in the app. Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID.

What about ads in the FREE version?
The FREE version includes clean, minimal advertising to support development:
  • Small banner ad at the bottom of the screen
  • Occasional interstitial ad (not overwhelming)
  • Ads are family-friendly via Google AdMob
Upgrade to PRO for a completely ad-free experience.
Can I use NestPlan without creating an account?
Yes! NestPlan does not require account creation or login. All your data stays on your device, ensuring maximum privacy.
How do I cancel my PRO subscription?
PRO subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. To cancel:
  1. Open Settings on your iOS device
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Find NestPlan and tap "Cancel Subscription"
